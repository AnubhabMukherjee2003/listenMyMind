ADHD

Between 7.8 and 11% of children aged 4 to 17 are diagnosed with ADHD any given year. ADHD affects people of all ages and includes multiple symptoms such as inability to concentrate, forgetfulness, inability to sit still, restlessness, and losing things" "An individual's symptoms vary depending on t heir age, gender, and type of ADHD: combined type ADHD, impulsive/hyperactive type ADHD, and inattentive and distractible type ADHD." Most people think meds are the only way to control ADHD. Well, many people diagnosed with the disorder find relief by using a combination of medications, life coaching, education, and talk therapy..
